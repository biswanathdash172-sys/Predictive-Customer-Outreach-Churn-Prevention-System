Core Capabilities

Built for Banking Operations

Every feature is designed around the daily workflow of Relationship Managers, Branch Managers, and Credit Officers — with language familiar to banking professionals.

🎯
Churn Risk Scoring
Every customer receives a daily churn probability (0–100%) from transaction history, digital engagement, product holding, and competitive outflow signals via NACH/UPI.
📞
RM Pre-Call Briefing
Before contacting an at-risk customer, your RM gets a tailored briefing — talking points, likely objections, and the best offer to make — generated by Claude AI in seconds.
😠
Complaint Intelligence
Complaints are automatically analysed for anger level, urgency, and churn intent. High-risk complaints are escalated with resolution priority scoring before the customer escalates.
⚡
Trigger Event Alerts
When a customer breaks an FD prematurely, creates a UPI mandate to a competitor, or reroutes salary — an alert fires instantly with a recommended RM action.
🎁
Next Best Product
The system recommends the product most likely to improve retention — a pre-approved home loan, special FD rate, or credit card upgrade — personalised per customer profile.
🏦
Branch Morning Report
Each branch receives a daily alert showing which at-risk customers may visit that day, their risk tier, and recommended action for front-desk staff and the branch RM.
🕵️
Competitor Intelligence
Tracks cross-bank outflows via NACH and UPI to identify which competitor is attracting your customers, broken down by city, product type, and branch region.
📈
Retention Analytics
Cohort retention curves show how well each campaign retained customers at 30, 60, and 90 days — segmented by product, channel, and customer segment for management review.
✉️
Personalised Outreach
Claude generates two message variants per customer in their preferred language and channel — WhatsApp, SMS, push, or email — with full RBI compliance checking before dispatch.
The PS4 Workflow

From Customer Signal to Retention — 8 Stages

The platform runs a continuous pipeline that turns raw banking data into a saved customer relationship.

01
Data Ingestion
Transaction history, digital engagement, product lifecycle, and competitive signals collected daily.
02
Risk Scoring
XGBoost assigns churn probability (0–100%) and predicts departure horizon (30/60/90 days).
03
Segmentation
Customers grouped into Price-Sensitive, Disengaged, Life-Event, Complaint-Driven, Product Maturity.
04
AI Message
Claude generates personalised retention offers with RBI misselling compliance verification.
05
Channel Dispatch
Sent via WhatsApp, SMS, push, email, or RM call — whichever has the highest open rate for that customer.
06
Response Tracking
Delivery, open, click, and acceptance tracked. Non-responses escalate to next channel after 72 hours.
07
Feedback Loop
Actual churn outcomes at 30/60/90 days retrain the model monthly for continuous improvement.
08
Dashboard
Live KPIs, RM leaderboard, city heatmaps, and exportable management reports for weekly reviews.
Banking Terms Explained

Familiar Language for Bank Staff

PS4 uses terminology your team already knows. Here is how each banking concept maps into the system.

KYC
Customer Risk Profile
Just like KYC verifies identity, PS4 builds a 360° behavioural profile using transaction frequency, product holdings, and complaint history to assess churn risk.
NPA
Churn Risk Score
A customer with risk score above 0.65 is treated like a potential NPA — flagged early, actioned promptly, and escalated to the RM before the loss materialises.
CLV
Customer Lifetime Value
The estimated total revenue a customer will generate. High CLV customers are prioritised first in the outreach queue — similar to prioritising top-tier borrowers.
NACH
Competitor Outflow Signal
When a new NACH or UPI mandate flows to a competitor bank, PS4 treats it as a strong churn signal — equivalent to a customer pre-closing their CASA account.
CSAT
Satisfaction Score
CSAT below 2.5/5 combined with complaint history is treated as a high-risk combination requiring immediate RM intervention and senior management attention.
FD
Fixed Deposit Signal
Premature FD breakage is one of the strongest churn signals in the model. The system immediately recommends a special renewal rate offer via the customer's preferred channel.
CASA
Core Account Indicator
Zero CASA transactions for 60+ days automatically flags a customer as Disengaged segment with a recommended push notification re-engagement campaign.
DND
Do Not Disturb Registry
The TRAI DND registry is checked before every outreach dispatch. Opted-out customers are automatically blocked — no exceptions, no manual override.
